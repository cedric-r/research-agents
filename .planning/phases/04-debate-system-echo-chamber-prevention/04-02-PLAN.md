## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-07 | Spoofing | Score table shows incorrect quality score due to missing validation | mitigate | All scores are clamped 0-10 in parseScoringJson() (Plan 1). Score table reads from validated data only. |
| T-04-08 | DoS | Empty narrative causes blank output section | mitigate | Fallback to "[No narrative reasoning provided by the judge.]" when narrative is empty. |
